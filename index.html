INSTRUCTIONS, STEP BY STEP

IN JUST FIVE STEPS TO YOUR PERFECT STORY

STEP 1

On the screen, you'll find three input windows. Starting from the left, the first window prompts you to enter a title for your story. Once you've entered a title, you can proceed to step 2.


STEP 2

In the second input window, you select a genre for your story, such as horror, comedy, fantasy, and so on. Once you've made your selection, you can proceed to step 3.


STEP 3

In the third window, you define the reading length of your text. This is limited to a maximum of 30 minutes.


STEP 4

In the last input window, press the "Generate" button and your story will come to life.
